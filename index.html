The Power of Determination

Determination is a central theme in Undertale, representing the will to keep going despite challenges. It's what drives the characters and the player in their journey through the Underground.

Key Features

Undertale is known for its unique combat system, where players can choose to spare or attack monsters. This choice system is central to the game's narrative, as it can lead to different outcomes, including the ability to complete the game without killing any monsters.

The game also features a rich cast of characters, each with their own unique personalities and stories. Players will encounter both friends and foes as they navigate the Underground.
